Alfredo Sauce

Description

Once you try this homemade Alfredo sauce recipe, you’ll never go back to the jarred stuff.

Ingredients:

  • ¼ cup butter

  • 1 cup heavy cream

  • 1 ½ cups freshly grated Parmesan cheese

  • 1 clove garlic, crushed

  • ¼ cup chopped fresh parsley

Steps:

  1. Gather all ingredients.



  2. Melt butter in a medium saucepan over low heat. Stir in cream and simmer for 5 minutes.





  3. Add cheese and garlic; whisk continuously until heated through. Stir in parsley and serve.





  4. Serve immediately and enjoy!
